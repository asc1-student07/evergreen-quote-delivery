## Who is the customer?
This is for customers that want to get a quote fast, with no commitment, no waiting, and no pressure.  Target audience would be for insurance first-timers, but no limited.

## What pain does Evergreen Quote remove?
The pain that is removed is time and rigidity.  Have a site that can give you a quote in seconds vs needing to create accounts, work with account managers, etc will drive engagement and reputation.  Having a responsive application that looks good everywhere gives the customers maximum flexibility with when, where and how they interact.

## What does "good" look like at end of the week?
- **Accuracy** Accurate quotes every time
- **Responsive** No matter what device you use to interact, the site always presents viewable information
-  **Brand** The site has full brand awareness to drive recognition

## What are we explicitly NOT doing this week?
- No backend.  The site is transient.
- No custom rating.  All users use the same calculation for premium.
- No account creation. Since the site is transient, no user info is created or stored.
- With no accounts, etc, that means no actual purchase or payments to policies.

## How will we know if it worked?
- The site visibly tests well
- Tests for all calculations / different insurance types are positive
